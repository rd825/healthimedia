Who We Are

About Us

HEALTHiMEDIA is a full-service production company devoted to branded storytelling. We breathe life into your brand by creating unique and personal stories that are authentic, creative and inspirational. Whether it’s a story about living with MS, training for an Ironman Triathlon, or crafting Parmigiano Reggiano, we produce narratives that move people and inspire action.

With over 20 years of storytelling experience, we’ve worked with doctors, patients, chefs, athletes, politicians and kids; and we’ve written and produced news and documentaries, marketing videos and stories for the web. Our clients include PBS, the Learning Channel, NBC, Barnabas Health, Everyday Health, and many other corporations and non-profits. And our work has garnered us Emmy Awards, Telly’s, and an Edward R. Murrow award.

Our Services

Our services start from the minute we sit down with a client. We get to know you and your company, your expectations, your hopes, and your budget. We listen. And then we come up with a plan of action for how best to tell your story.

Our clients are included every step of the way from scheduling to scripting, to interviewing, shooting and editing. We aim for no surprises… except the good kind, when you realize we have exceeded your expectations. Our services include:

  • Videography (HD Cameras, Go-Pro HD, Lighting Packages)
  • Producer/Director Services
  • Script writing
  • Interviewing
  • Avid digital editing
  • Voice over services
  • Editorial services
  • DVD authoring/mastering/duplication
  • Web and social media distribution
